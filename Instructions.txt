This is the data analysis coding notebook for one of the most famous ride sharing companies of bangladesh called pathao. This analysis is done as a part of the capstone project for the product management career track by bohubrihi

How the analysis is being done:

Using the python based api to find the general application statistics for the pathao application
Finding out the comments on the application at bulk,
Finding out the 5 star and the 4 star reviews to find out the strengths and the opportunities for the product,
Finding out the 1 and 2 star reviews to find out the weakness and the problems with the product,
Finally do a overall sentiment analysis of the comments to find out the general perception about the product.
